Welcome to Ascent by Oyster, a free virtual conference where anyone passionate about distributed work—founders and C-level executives, People Ops pros, and knowledge workers around the globe—connect to change the world of work, together.

This year, our focus is on navigating distributed work—and life beyond work too.
